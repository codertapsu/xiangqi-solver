## What we collect and why

- **Board screenshots / images you analyze.** When you use **Cloud (our service)**
  mode, the image of the board you choose to analyze is sent to our server, which
  forwards it to **OpenAI** for board recognition. Images are used only to compute the
  move and are **not** used to identify you. [State your retention: e.g. "images are
  processed in memory and not stored," or your actual policy.]
- **An anonymous account + hint balance.** To meter free hints, rewarded-ad hints, and
  purchases, the app creates an **anonymous account** tied to a randomly generated
  device identifier (not your name, email, or Google account). We store your hint
  balance and a transaction ledger on our server.
- **Purchase information.** When you buy a hint pack, **Google Play** processes the
  payment; we receive a purchase token/order id (not your card details) to verify the
  purchase and credit hints.
- **Advertising data.** We show **rewarded ads** via **Google AdMob**, which may collect
  your device's advertising identifier and related data to serve and measure ads,
  subject to your consent (we show a consent form where required, e.g. in the EEA/UK).
- **Your own OpenAI API key (optional).** If you choose **"Use my own key"** mode, your
  key is stored **only on your device** (in the platform keystore) and used to call
  OpenAI directly. It is never sent to our server.

The app also requests screen-capture and overlay permissions **only** to capture the
board you point it at, with your explicit consent each session; captures are not taken
in the background.
